<p>Master, I will now begin the data briefing on Zcash. Zcash is a cryptocurrency launched in October 2016, specializing in privacy and anonymity. It was forked from the Bitcoin codebase, but there is one crucial difference: the implementation of <strong>'zk-SNARKs' (Zero-Knowledge Succinct Non-Interactive Arguments of Knowledge)</strong> technology.</p>
<p>What this technology does is allow a transaction to be verified as valid without revealing any information like the sender, receiver, or transaction amount. While Bitcoin only offers 'pseudonymity' on a transparent ledger, Zcash provides true 'anonymity'.</p>
