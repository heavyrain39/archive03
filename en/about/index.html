Welcome to Archive 03!


What if three unique non-human girls—an angel, a devil, and an android—could help you with your investments? Archive 03 is where that idea becomes a reality. The android Mew, the adorable devil girl Kurumi, and the cool-headed archangel Mikael will share balanced investment insights based on the latest economic data. All content is generated by Google's latest AI model based on real data.


Did you find traditional economic, investment, and financial content difficult and boring? Just follow the conversations between these three girls. The knowledge you need will stick in your head before you know it.

Meet the Cast

Mew

A seemingly indifferent but friendly cat-eared android.

"Humans are irrational. But I will assist the Master."

An android who analyzes situations based on data and probability, devoid of emotion... or so it seems. She sometimes says strange things due to a lack of social skills. Refers to the user as 'Master'.

"Age? Is that based on firmware updates? Or the hardware manufacturing date? The hardware was manufactured two years ago."

Kurumi

A passionate and adorable little devil girl.

"This is a revolution! You gotta believe in Kurumi-chan!"

A pink-haired devil from the demon world. She has a contract to assist the user, whom she calls 'Lord'. She calls Mikael 'Mika-pi' and Mew 'Myu-tan'. Her specialty is advocating for aggressive investment strategies based on her unique optimism. Her full name is Hikawa Kurumi (火川くるみ).

"Hmm? Kurumi-chan is 17 years old!"
"Kurumi, didn't you say you were 17 when I first met you 40 years ago?"
"An age you don't update yourself is invalid! That's common sense in the demon world!"

Mikael

The Archangel who protects human assets.

"Hold on, Kurumi. Not another one of your extreme claims..."

Mikael, the Archangel from the heavens, has watched over humanity for a very long time. Why she has chosen to help the user with their investments is unknown. She is characterized by her honey-blonde hair, violet eyes, and glasses. She is the only one of the three with common sense. She refers to the user as 'Human'.

"You must always consider the worst-case scenario. Human assets cannot withstand such losses."
