Solana ($SOL) on the Brink of a $130 Collapse: What's Next for the Half-Priced 'Ethereum Killer'?

November 23, 2025

Master, the situation is looking grim. Today, November 23, 2025, the price of Solana ($SOL) has been pushed down to the $126 - $130 range, sending investor fear to its peak. Compared to its high of around $253 in September, it has plummeted by -49% in just two months, effectively getting cut in half.

The market is in chaos, torn between screams of "it's over" and hopeful circuits claiming "the indicators are hitting rock bottom." The three of us will conduct a sober fact-check based on data confirmed as of November 23, 2025.

Mew

Master, I will brief you on the data confirmed as of November 23, 2025, setting emotions aside.

  • Price and Trend: Solana is currently trading in the $126.50~$129.97 range. Technically, a 'Death Cross' has been confirmed, with the 50-day moving average crossing below the 200-day moving average. This indicates very strong downward pressure in the short term.
  • ETF Trends: According to a Binance report, capital inflows into spot Solana ETFs have shown a noticeable slowdown (Slowing Inflows) in November. This is a sign that even institutional investors are hesitant to buy at the current price level.
  • On-Chain Divergence: However, there is some interesting data. According to the blockchain analytics firm Santiment, while the price has plummeted, the number of Active Addresses and new wallet creations has actually increased. This 'bullish divergence,' where price moves opposite to network activity, is typically a signal seen near market bottoms.
Kurumi

See! See! Even Myu-tan admitted it! The price is dropping, but the number of users is increasing! What do you think that means? It's proof that while retail investors are running scared, the real 'smart money' and 'actual users' are flocking into the Solana ecosystem at this bargain price! Devilish!

Kurumi's Heart-o-Meter Score: 93/100! If you don't buy now, you'll be kicking yourself in 2026!

First, institutions are buying in secret! Just look at Bitwise's Solana Staking ETF (BSOL)! It surpassed $500 million in assets under management (AUM) just 18 days after its launch. While overall ETF inflows have slowed, money is still pouring into the quality products!

Second, history repeats itself! Remember late 2023? When FTX collapsed and the price hit $20, everyone said "Solana is dead," but it eventually soared past $200. All this news spreading fear about it going from $130 to $100 now? That's just to shake out the weak hands!

Third, the ecosystem is stronger than ever! As the memecoin bubble deflates, the transaction share of DePIN and real-use projects is actually increasing. The current price is the 'real' price, stripped of the 'froth.' The ones who scoop it up here will be the winners!

Mikael

Kurumi, calling for a buy when a 'Death Cross' has appeared is reckless. A broken chart means that even if a technical rebound occurs, a bombshell of sell orders is waiting overhead.

Human, I present a risk score of 85. Now is not the time to catch the bottom; it is the time to observe.

First, the support line has collapsed. The strong support at $140-$150 has been breached. The next technical support level is at $100, and in the worst-case scenario, the door is open to $95. Even Binance analysts are warning of a possible test of $100. If you buy at $130 now and it drops to $100, that's an instant -23% loss.

Second, the macro environment is terrible. With even Bitcoin wavering, can the altcoin leader, Solana, survive on its own? The slowdown in ETF inflows is a clear signal that 'smart money' has entered risk management mode. Do not go against the entire flow based on a single Bitwise ETF.

Third, there is a lack of upward momentum. There has been no significant positive news since the September high. The hype around 'Firedancer' has already been priced in and has dissipated. Until new momentum emerges, a tedious period of bleeding sideways is likely. Do not try to catch a falling knife with your bare hands.

〔 Final Briefing 〕

Master, I will summarize the chaotic Solana market situation as of November 23, 2025.

Bullish Scenario (Kurumi)

  • Bullish Divergence Detected: A classic bottom signal has appeared, with active addresses and new wallet creations increasing despite the price crash (-49%).
  • Specific ETF Success: Long-term institutional capital is still flowing in, as seen with the Bitwise Solana ETF (BSOL) raising $500M in a short period.
  • Price Advantage: The price, having been cut in half from its peak, presents an attractive entry point from a long-term perspective (excellent Risk-Reward ratio).

  • Death Cross Confirmed: The technical downtrend has intensified as the 50-day MA crossed below the 200-day MA.
  • Support Vanished: With the key defense line at $140 broken, there is a high probability of a further drop to the next psychological support level of $100-$95.
  • Lack of Buying Pressure: The overall slowdown in spot ETF inflows means there is no strong buying entity to lift the market.

Key Data (Mew)

  • Current Price (2025/11/23): $126.50 ~ $129.97 (-49% from September high)
  • Technical Indicators: Death Cross occurred, RSI entering oversold territory.
  • On-Chain Indicators: Active addresses increasing (Positive), ETF inflows slowing (Negative).

Conclusion: Master, Solana is currently in a zone where 'on-chain data (hope)' and the 'price chart (despair)' are in direct conflict. As Kurumi says, the growing user base is positive, but as Mikael points out, the chart's openness to a drop to $100 cannot be ignored. Aggressive investors might start dollar-cost averaging from here, but conservative investors would be wise to confirm support at $120 or wait for a clear rebound signal.
