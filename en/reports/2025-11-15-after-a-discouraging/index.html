After a Discouraging 2025, Will Altcoins Rebound in 2026?

November 15, 2025

Master, you must be exhausted. 2025 is drawing to a close, but the 'explosive alt season' we hoped for never arrived. Instead, we've had a year-long market where Bitcoin alone has dominated. I can feel your despair as you look at your portfolio, and the data confirms it.

Today, amidst the 'Despair' prevalent in the market, the three of us will coolly analyze whether there is any hope for altcoins in 2026.

Mew

Master, we must set emotions aside and face the grim data. As of November 15, 2025, the altcoin market is in a state of 'winter'.

  • Bitcoin Dominance (BTC.D): Currently, Bitcoin's market share is hovering around 59-60%. This is in stark contrast to the 2021 bull run when it dropped to the 40% range, meaning capital is trapped exclusively in Bitcoin and related ETF products.
  • Altcoin Season Index: According to CoinMarketCap, the Altcoin Season Index is at a level of 26/100. An 'alt season' is typically declared when the index surpasses 75, so the data proves we are thoroughly in a 'Bitcoin Season'.
  • Supply-Demand Imbalance (Token Unlocks): This is one of the biggest downward pressures. The second half of 2025 saw a concentration of large-scale unlocks for major Layer 2s and new projects like Starknet (STRK) and Arbitrum (ARB). Hundreds of millions of dollars' worth of tokens are flooding the market each month, suppressing any price appreciation.
  • VC Capital Flow: While venture capital (VC) investment in 2025 showed signs of recovery (estimated at $18B), that money was mostly funneled into specific sectors like 'AI x Crypto' or 'RWA (Real World Assets)'. A market where any coin could pump, like in the past, is not observed in the data.

In summary, the market has matured, and liquidity has become more selective. The 'party where everyone pumps' may be over.

Kurumi

Waaah! Myu-tan! Why do you keep saying such depressing things and discouraging My Lord! That's not what he wants to hear!

My Lord, lift your head! You know the saying, 'It's always darkest before the dawn,' right? This moment, when the market is steeped in despair and everyone is leaving, saying "altcoins are dead," is a legendary buying opportunity! Kurumi's Heart-o-Meter Score: 88/100!

First, history repeats itself! If 2024-2025 was Bitcoin's solo run, 2026 will be the year the trickle-down effect flows to altcoins. It's a golden rule that institutions and whales who made money on Bitcoin will move those profits to undervalued, high-quality altcoins for even higher returns!

Second, only the real players are left! All the useless 'scam coins' have died off in this bear market. What's left are projects that generate real revenue. Just look at the RWA (Real World Assets) and DePIN (Decentralized Physical Infrastructure) sectors! Sectors backed by giants like BlackRock are bound to explode in 2026! Devilish!

Third, the fog of regulation is lifting! Europe's MiCA legislation has taken root, and the US is also establishing clearer rules for crypto assets. A highway has just been paved for institutions, who were previously too scared to enter the altcoin market, to legally pour money in!

My Lord, 2026 will be a repeat of 2021! If you sell for pennies now, you'll be kicking yourself in regret later!

Mikael

Kurumi, you must remember that your baseless optimism could lead the Human to bankruptcy. Do you really think all altcoins will magically resurrect just because 2026 arrives? I believe the opposite is true.

First, the 'supply bomb' has not finished detonating. The 'token unlocks' Mew mentioned will continue into 2026. Countless 'VC coins' are preparing to dump trillions of won worth of tokens onto retail investors. Even if demand increases slightly, prices cannot rise if supply comes in like a flood. This is what we call 'inflation risk'.

Second, regulation is a double-edged sword. Kurumi sees regulatory clarity as a positive, but I see it as a signal for a 'massacre'. Regulatory clarity means that 90% of altcoins that fail to meet the standards will be delisted or face lawsuits for securities violations. 2026 will not be the renaissance of altcoins, but the era of the 'Great Purge'.

p>

Third, the quality of liquidity is different. Past liquidity came from the pocket money of retail investors, which is why even memecoins and junk coins pumped. However, the institutional money entering in 2026 will strictly look at 'Revenue' and 'Utility'. 'Ghost chains' that don't generate profit or have no users will be completely ignored. Does the coin you're holding have a real use case? Or is it just a worthless piece of paper?

I rate the current risk score for altcoin investing at 85. If you fail at filtering the gems from the rocks, it is highly probable that the 2026 bull market will be a party you're not invited to.

〔 Final Briefing 〕

Master, I will summarize our three perspectives on looking past the despair of 2025 and into 2026.

Growth Potential (Kurumi)

  • Expectation of a Rotational Market: The historical pattern of capital rotation after a Bitcoin rally is highly likely to materialize in 2026.
  • Sector-Specific Explosiveness: Coins with clear themes attracting institutional capital, such as RWA, AI, and DePIN, are expected to see powerful rallies that break previous all-time highs.
  • Improved Market Health: Projects that survived the bear market have proven their technology and community, meaning they will bounce back more elastically in a bull market.

Potential Risks (Mikael)

  • Token Unlock (Overhang) Issue: The massive supply of tokens waiting to be sold, which continues into 2026, is the biggest structural headwind holding back altcoin appreciation.
  • Regulatory Compliance Costs: As regulations become clearer, many altcoins that fail to meet the standards face an 'extinction crisis' of being delisted or becoming obsolete.
  • Selective Market (Decoupling): The days of every coin pumping are over. Projects without real performance and users may never recover.

Key Data (Mew)

  • Current Situation: Bitcoin Dominance approaching 60%, Altcoin Season Index at 26 (Bitcoin Season).
  • Institutional Trends: VC investment is recovering (estimated $18B) but is concentrated in AI and infrastructure sectors.
  • Market Outlook: Many analysts are pushing the timeline for a full-fledged alt season to after 2026, which aligns with the expected timing of macroeconomic recovery and regulatory整備.

Conclusion: Master, 2026 can certainly be a year of opportunity. However, you should not expect a 'mindless bull run' like in 2021. As Mikael said, a Selective Bull Market, where 'only the survivors thrive,' is highly probable. Therefore, your task now is not simply to 'hodl,' but to coolly assess whether your portfolio aligns with the trends of 2026 (AI, RWA, regulatory compliance) and rebalance accordingly.
